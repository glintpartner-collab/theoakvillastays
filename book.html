Before You Book

Booking FAQs

Do I need to pay in advance? +
For stays under 7 nights, no advance payment is required. You can pay on arrival via UPI or cash. For 7+ night stays, we request 30% advance to confirm the booking.
What is the cancellation policy? +
Free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in. For cancellations within 24 hours, one night's charge applies. For advance payments on long stays, 50% refund within 24 hours of booking.
Are all meals really included? +
Yes — breakfast, lunch, and dinner are cooked fresh daily by our kitchen team and included in every package at zero extra cost. No restaurant bills, no meal planning.
What are the check-in and check-out times? +
Check-in from 12:00 noon. Check-out by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged subject to availability — just message us in advance.
Can I visit The Oak Villa before booking? +
Yes. WhatsApp us to arrange a quick visit to see the floor, the view, and meet our team before confirming. We're happy to show you around.
Is the property available year-round? +
Yes. The Oak Villa is open 365 days a year. Dehradun's year-round climate (avg 25°C) makes it ideal for any month. Peak season is April-June and October-December — book early for these months.
