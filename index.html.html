Our Story

Where home cooking
meets Himalayan light

The Oak Villa sits on Rajpur Road — Dehradun's most sought-after address — on a quiet private floor with an unobstructed view of the Mussoorie hills. We started as a food operation serving 48,000+ meals across the city. The homestay was the natural next step.

The concept is simple: a fully private 1BHK floor, every meal cooked fresh by our kitchen team, and a balcony where you watch the hills glow gold at dawn. No shared spaces. No restaurant bills. No planning.

Most Dehradun stays give you a room and a direction to the nearest dhaba. We give you the dhaba inside, the view outside, and the whole floor to yourself.
